How We Do It

Making people money does no good if their computer is slowed down or damaged. That's why we have designed our software to only run when people are not using thier computer and when it is plugged in.

When our software runs, we use your computer to solve complex probles which organizations pay to have solved. Because your machine is doing the work, you get paid!

Why We Do It?

We believe the world would be a better place if everyone was free to do what they loved. The reason this does not happen is because people's financial obligations imprison them into the job that pays the most, not what they love the most. To free everyone to do what they love, we need to free them from the chains of their finanicial obligations. Chedr is the tool to break those chains.
