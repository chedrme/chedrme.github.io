How We Do It

Making people money does no good if their computer is slowed down or damaged. That's why we have designed our software to only run when people are not using their computer and when it is plugged in.

When our software runs, we use your computer to solve complex problems which organizations pay to have solved. Because your machine is doing the work, you get paid!
